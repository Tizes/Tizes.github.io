Fresh details Serious Sam 4 graphics and technologies

It is known that in the production of the shooter used photogrammetry technology and motion capture. Due to the success puzzle The Talos Principle, in Serious Sam 4 will be some new and unexpected features. Croteam going back to spoil the game for anyone who downloads a pirated copy .
